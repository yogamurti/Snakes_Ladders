Snakes & Ladders

The first person to have a score of 100 or higher wins!

If your new score (after a roll) will be the same score as another player, your previous roll will be invalid and you will need to roll again.

There are 2 snakes and 2 ladders:
Snakes: 20 leads to 10 and 80 leads to 70.
Ladders: 40 leads to 45 and 60 leads to 65.
